About Me

Me talking about Me

I'm a fourth year student in Seneca who is currently in search of opportunities in the working environment where I can extend my knowledge and at the same time become a valuable member of the community/company. I have had a passion for programming ever since the first year I had got into the Bachelor Degree of Software Development program. With this passion, I'm anticipating for new challenges to encounter.

Throughout the past four years, I have learned a wide variety of programming knowledge from the most fundamental to an intermediate level. I started off learning the structure of programming using C/C++ programming, which gave me a strong base of coding knowledge such as addresses and pointers, different methods of structuring algorithms, I/O usage, and polymorphism. I then applied these knowledge into web programming languages such as PHP, (CGI)Perl, and C#(ASP.Net). Recently, I have also been commiting myself to learn Java, which has given me an even stronger base knowledge on Object Oriented structures. Through out this whole process, I have also learned many associated topics including database (such as MSSQL, Oracle, and MySQL), Rational Rose, networking administration, and business analyst.

As I must re-enforce the amount of passion I have for programming, I bring to your attention that I have grew an extra strong passion for C#. Since I have started learning C# coding, I have started to spend many of my break time working on websites and learning more new techniques on website development.

As a Software Developer, I strive for perfection in both code and knowledge, and I am prepare for any challenges ahead of me.
